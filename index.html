  1. About

    Objective

    To obtain a teaching position at the secondary level where I can apply my unique education and previous experience working with diverse populations as a teacher and counselor to contribute to the achievement of the school mission and student objectives.

    Current Accomplishments

    I promote digital citizenship in my 7th and 8th grade pre-engineering courses, addressing the lifelong skills that they need for success in our data driven world: accountability and responsibility, working independently and interdependently, and being inclusive of all classmates in their school community. I have worked to create a pre-engineering curriculum that targets the state competencies in a way that allowed for cross-curricular instruction. Students learn how to apply the engineering design process to various projects and problem solve creatively through a mostly self-paced curriculum.

    Present and Future Aspirations

    Looking forward with a growth mindset, I am going to continue to diversify my endorsements to promote understanding and better meet the needs of my students. This includes completing my coursework in gifted education as well as the testing required for K - 12 mathermatics.
